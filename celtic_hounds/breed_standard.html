Breed Strandard

September 12, 1950, the stansrds were defined for the Irish Wolfhound. These standards are used for ruling dogs in competition. The Irish Wolfhounds is the largest and tallest of the sight hounds. They are know for their power and swiftness. Irish Wolfhounds can stand up on their hind legs and can measure about 7 ft tall.
