Irish Wolfhounds

Website Purpose:

The purpose of this website to educate pet owner's on the pro's and con's of owning an Irish Wolfhound. Provide the basic infomation on the breed, what their health problems are and how to provides them with the highest quality of life. Also the cost of owning one the largest dog breeds.

Intended Audience:

The intended audience is anyone who wants to know more about the Irish Wolfhound breed and anyone who might want to adopt an Irish Wolfhound.

Content:

The content of this page will contain the history of the breed, health infomation. I will also include pictures, video and websites for adoption/rescue organizations.
